## Hiring Strategy

### Talent Sourcing

| Strategy | Markets | Roles Targeted |
|---|---|---|
| **Jordan talent hub** | Amman | Engineering, operations, affiliate management |
| **KSA talent** | Riyadh | Sales, BD, KSA market roles |
| **Egypt talent** | Cairo | Market entry team, operations, content |
| **Remote/Global** | Remote | Senior engineers, AI/ML specialists |
| **UAE local** | Abu Dhabi, Dubai | Executive team, client-facing roles |

### Why MENA Hiring Is a Competitive Advantage

| Factor | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Cost efficiency** | Senior engineers in Amman at $4–6K/mo vs $15–25K/mo in SF/London |
| **Arabic fluency** | Essential for MENA platform — built into hiring, not added later |
| **Time zone alignment** | Team in GMT+2 to GMT+3 — aligned with MENA clients |
| **Cultural understanding** | Team inherently understands regional business norms |
| **Growing tech talent** | Jordan, Egypt, KSA producing strong engineering graduates |
