## Unit Economics

| Metric | Current | Target (Post-Funding) | Industry Benchmark |
|--------|---------|----------------------|-------------------|
| **Average Revenue Per Client (ARPC)** | ~$2,000/month | $3,500/month | $1,500-5,000 (affiliate platforms) |
| **Client Acquisition Cost (CAC)** | ~$3,000 | ~$4,000 | $3K-$10K (B2B AdTech) |
| **Client Lifetime Value (LTV)** | ~$50,000+ | ~$80,000+ | Based on 87% retention, ~4yr avg lifetime |
| **LTV:CAC Ratio** | ~16:1 | ~20:1 | Best-in-class: >3:1 ([SaaS Capital](https://www.saascapital.com/)) |
| **Gross Margin** | ~65% | ~75%+ | Affiliate platforms: 60-80% |
| **Net Revenue Retention** | ~110% | ~120%+ | Top quartile: >110% |
| **Publisher Acquisition Cost** | Very low | < $50 | 64% of publishers join organically |
| **Take Rate** | 15-25% of brand commission | 15-25% | Industry: 10-30% |

### Unit Economics Assumptions
- Average brand spends $4,000-$10,000/month on affiliate campaigns
- Perff AI takes 15-25% of the brand's commission payout as platform fee
- Average client lifetime: 3.5+ years (based on 87% annual retention)
- 64% of publishers acquire organically → very low blended publisher acquisition cost
- Gross margin improves as platform automates more managed services

## Key Assumptions

1. **MENA e-commerce continues 25%+ annual growth** — supported by Saudi Vision 2030 and UAE digital transformation initiatives
2. **Affiliate marketing adoption in MENA follows global trends** with 3-5 year lag — currently at ~5% of digital marketing spend vs. 16% globally
3. **AI features create defensible moat** — fraud detection and budget optimization become harder to replicate as training data grows
4. **KSA represents 40%+ of MENA e-commerce TAM** — primary expansion vector
5. **Platform economics improve at scale** — managed services decrease as % of revenue; SaaS and performance fees increase
6. **Publisher network effects** — more publishers attract more brands, which attract more publishers (flywheel)
