## Total Addressable Market (TAM) — $30B

**Definition:** The total global affiliate marketing industry value by 2031.

The global affiliate marketing industry was valued at approximately **$17B in 2023** and is projected to grow to **$30B by 2031**, representing a **7.7% CAGR**.

### Key Data Points

| Source | Metric | Value |
|---|---|---|
| Astute Analytica (2024) | Global market size 2023 | $17.1B |
| Astute Analytica (2024) | Global market size 2031 | $31.3B |
| Statista (2024) | Affiliate marketing spend (US only) 2023 | $9.56B |
| IAB (2024) | Performance marketing share of digital ad spend | 16% globally |
| Grand View Research (2024) | CAGR 2024–2031 | 7.7% |

### Growth Drivers
- **Shift to performance-based models** — Brands moving from CPM/CPC to CPA/CPS
- **E-commerce acceleration** — Post-pandemic digital commerce growth sustained
- **Creator economy** — Content creators becoming significant distribution channels
- **AI/ML adoption** — Better attribution, optimization, and fraud detection
- **Privacy changes** — Cookie deprecation pushing brands toward first-party, intent-based channels

## Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM) — ~$1.5B

**Definition:** The total MENA performance marketing spend — the portion of the global market that Perff AI can theoretically serve.

### MENA Digital Advertising Landscape

| Market | Digital Ad Spend (2024) | Growth Rate |
|---|---|---|
| UAE | $1.8B | 14% YoY |
| KSA | $3.2B | 18% YoY |
| Egypt | $0.9B | 22% YoY |
| Rest of MENA | $1.6B | 12% YoY |
| **Total MENA** | **$7.5B** | **15% avg YoY** |

### Performance Marketing as % of Digital

Globally, performance marketing (affiliate, CPA, lead gen) accounts for approximately **15–20%** of total digital ad spend. Applying a conservative **15%** to MENA's $7.5B digital ad spend:

**MENA Performance Marketing SAM ≈ $1.1–1.5B**

This number is growing **25%+ annually** as MENA brands increasingly adopt performance-based models.
