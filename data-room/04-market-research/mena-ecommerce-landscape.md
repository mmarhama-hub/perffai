## Key Market Characteristics

### Demographics
- **Population:** 400M+ across MENA
- **Median age:** 26 years (one of youngest globally)
- **Under 30:** 60%+ of population
- **Urban population:** 70%+
- **Internet penetration:** 70%+ (GCC: 95%+)
- **Smartphone penetration:** 75%+ (GCC: 90%+)

### Consumer Behavior
- **Mobile-first:** 70%+ of e-commerce transactions via mobile
- **Social commerce:** Growing rapidly — Instagram, TikTok, Snapchat driving purchases
- **Coupon culture:** Strong coupon/deal-seeking behavior across GCC
- **Cash-on-delivery:** Still significant (30–40%) but declining rapidly
- **Cross-border shopping:** 40%+ of purchases are cross-border
- **Arabic content preference:** 67% of consumers prefer Arabic-language shopping experiences
