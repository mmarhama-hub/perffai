## 1. Executive Summary

Perff AI is the **only AI-native performance marketing platform purpose-built for MENA**. Our go-to-market strategy is designed around a two-sided marketplace model — acquiring **enterprise brands** on the demand side and **publishers** on the supply side — while building category leadership in a market with no dominant incumbent.

### GTM Thesis

> **"Own MENA affiliate before the globals arrive."**

- There is no single dominant affiliate platform in MENA today.
- Global players (CJ, Rakuten, Impact.com) lack local infrastructure, publishers, and Arabic-language capabilities.
- Perff AI has a **12–18 month window** to establish insurmountable network effects before any credible competitor can replicate our publisher network, fraud data, and enterprise relationships.

### GTM Model

| Dimension | Approach |
|---|---|
| **Primary Motion** | Enterprise direct sales (consultative, relationship-driven) |
| **Secondary Motion** | Inbound content + thought leadership |
| **Supply Side** | Publisher recruitment via word-of-mouth + referral |
| **Expansion Path** | UAE/KSA (existing) → Egypt (2027) → Rest of MENA (2028) |
| **Product Evolution** | Managed service (now) → Self-serve SaaS (2026–2027) |

### Market Opportunity

| Scope | Value | Basis |
|---|---|---|
| **TAM** | $30B | Global affiliate marketing by 2031 |
| **SAM** | $1.5B | MENA performance marketing spend (2025) |
| **SOM** | $200M | UAE + KSA + Egypt (3-year capture) |

```
AWARENESS
  ↓  Website sessions, LinkedIn impressions, event reach, brand search volume
INTEREST
  ↓  Content downloads, blog reads, LinkedIn followers, publisher sign-up rate
CONSIDERATION
  ↓  Demo requests, inbound brand inquiries, publisher onboarding applications
INTENT
  ↓  Qualified meetings (SQLs), pilot requests, proposals submitted
CONVERSION
  ↓  Signed brand contracts, activated publishers
RETENTION
  ↓  Logo retention rate, NRR, publisher activity rate
EXPANSION
  ↓  Upsells, additional verticals/markets, referrals generated
```
