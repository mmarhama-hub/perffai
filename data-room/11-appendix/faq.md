## About Perff AI

### What is Perff AI?

Perff AI is an AI-powered affiliate marketing platform purpose-built for the MENA region. We connect brands with affiliate publishers — automating campaign matching, fraud detection, and performance optimization so brands pay only for real results.

**Think of it as:** "The Shopify of affiliate marketing for the Middle East" — instead of enabling stores, we enable performance-based partnerships between brands and publishers at scale.

"Perff" is derived from **"Performance"** — reflecting the company's core focus on performance-based marketing where outcomes are measured, optimized, and paid for based on results.

## Technology & Data

### How does Perff AI's fraud detection work?

Perff AI's fraud detection system analyzes **50+ signals per click event** in real-time:

- **IP analysis** — VPN detection, proxy identification, geo-consistency checks
- **Device fingerprinting** — Browser characteristics, device type, screen resolution
- **Behavioral signals** — Click velocity, session duration, page interaction patterns
- **Network analysis** — Click farm pattern detection, bot traffic identification
- **Historical patterns** — Cross-campaign fraud signature matching

The system is trained specifically on **MENA traffic patterns**, making it 3× more effective than global fraud tools on regional traffic.

## Market & Industry

### Why affiliate marketing? Why not social media or Google Ads?

| Channel | Model | Brand's Risk | Perff AI Advantage |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Google Ads** | Pay per click (CPC) | Pays for clicks, not conversions | — |
| **Social Media Ads** | Pay per impression (CPM) | Pays for eyeballs, not results | — |
| **Affiliate Marketing** | Pay per conversion (CPS/CPA) | **Pays only for actual results** | Zero risk for brands |

Affiliate marketing is the **only channel where brands have zero risk** — they pay only when a customer actually converts. This is why affiliate is the fastest-growing digital channel globally.
