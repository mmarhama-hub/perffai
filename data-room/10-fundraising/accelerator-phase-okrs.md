# Perff AI — Accelerator Phase OKRs (May–Aug 2026)

> One Objective, four measurable Key Results, execution timeline, and success indicators.

---

## Objective (O)

Launch and validate the self-serve, AI-assisted growth engine so brands can activate faster, convert better, and scale with lower operational friction.

---

## Key Results (KRs)

| KR | Starting Point | Target by Aug 2026 | Owner |
|---|---:|---:|---|
| KR1: Reduce brand campaign setup time | ~72 hours (operational baseline) | <48 hours for 80% of new campaigns | Product + CX |
| KR2: Improve conversion performance on pilot cohorts | CVR baseline 8.2% | 9.5%+ CVR on AI-assisted cohorts | Product + Data |
| KR3: Reduce invalid conversion / fraud impact | Current baseline from top campaigns | -20% invalid conversion rate | Engineering + Risk |
| KR4: Increase self-serve adoption among active brands | 0 brands on full self-serve workflow | 20 brands actively using self-serve workflow | Product + GTM |

---

## Execution Timeline

| Period | Execution Focus | KR Milestones |
|---|---|---|
| May 2026 | Build + instrument | KR1/KR4 baseline instrumentation complete |
| Jun 2026 | Beta launch | 10 brands on onboarding beta; setup-time reduction starts |
| Jul 2026 | AI + fraud rollout | AI recommendations v1 live; fraud v2 in shadow mode |
| Aug 2026 | Scale + validate | 20 self-serve brands; KR thresholds measured and signed off |

---

## Success Indicators

- Primary: 3 of 4 KRs hit by Aug 2026
- Quality gate: no material increase in payout disputes while scaling
- Commercial signal: improving NRR trend from self-serve pilot accounts
- Readiness signal: roadmap confidence for Q4 API/self-serve scale phase

## Weekly Tracking Format

- Progress review every Monday with KR % completion
- Confidence score per KR (1–10)
- Blockers + decision log
- Owner accountability and next actions
