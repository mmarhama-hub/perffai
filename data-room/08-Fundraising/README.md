## Investor FAQ

### Business Model

**Q: How does Perff AI make money?**
A: Three streams — (1) Performance fees: 15-25% of the commission brands pay to publishers, (2) Platform/SaaS fees: monthly subscriptions ($500-$2,000+/mo), (3) Managed services: premium white-glove campaign management for enterprise clients.

**Q: Why is an $8M round called "Pre-Seed"?**
A: Perff AI has been bootstrapped and revenue-generating since 2022. This is the company's first institutional funding round. The $8M reflects the capital needed to:
- Build a world-class AI/ML engineering team
- Expand into KSA and Egypt simultaneously
- Create a self-serve platform to scale beyond managed services

**Q: What's the path to profitability?**
A: At ~$3.5M revenue with 75%+ gross margin and a 25-person team, the company approaches breakeven. Expected by Month 18-24 post-funding.

### Market

**Q: Why MENA specifically?**
A: MENA e-commerce is growing 15-25% annually but affiliate marketing is at ~5% of digital ad spend (vs. 16% globally). No dedicated MENA affiliate platform exists at scale. Perff AI has first-mover advantage with 3+ years of data and relationships.

**Q: Can global players like CJ or Impact.com enter MENA?**
A: They have tried and failed to gain meaningful traction because:
- No Arabic support or Arabic-speaking communities
- No local publisher networks
- No MENA-specific fraud detection
- No local payment rails (AED, SAR, JOD, EGP)
- No managed services for relationship-driven MENA brands
Building this from scratch would take 3-5 years and significant investment.

### Product

**Q: Is the AI real or aspirational?**
A: Currently live in production: fraud detection MVP, predictive campaign scoring, automated reporting, and budget optimization engine. Post-funding: multi-layer fraud detection, publisher recommendation engine, adaptive budget allocation, self-serve dashboard, and API.

**Q: What prevents a competitor from copying the technology?**
A: The moat is data, not code. Perff AI has 3+ years of MENA-specific conversion data from 250+ campaigns and 870+ publishers. This training data is impossible for a new entrant to replicate. The network effects (publishers ↔ brands) create additional defensibility.

### Team

**Q: Why is this team uniquely positioned?**
A: Rima Hani has 10+ years running affiliate programs for major MENA brands (Equiti Group, DigiZag, Digital Advantage). She knows every major brand marketing director in the GCC. Fahad Alsaedi brings tech leadership with a successful exit and experience at Nana (KSA unicorn). The combination of deep domain expertise + proven tech execution is rare.
