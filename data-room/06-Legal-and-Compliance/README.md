## Regulatory Landscape

### UAE — Primary Market

| Regulation | Authority | Relevance |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| **Federal Decree-Law No. 45/2021 (Personal Data Protection)** | UAE Data Office | Governs personal data collection, processing, and cross-border transfer — applies to publisher and brand data |
| **Consumer Protection Law** | Ministry of Economy | Advertising transparency, fair trading practices, affiliate disclosure requirements |
| **E-Commerce Law (Federal Decree-Law No. 14/2023)** | TRA | Regulates online transactions, electronic contracts, and digital commerce platforms |
| **Anti-Fraud Regulations** | Various | Criminal penalties for digital fraud — supports Perff AI's fraud detection mission |
| **Free Zone Regulations** | ADGM / DIFC / Other | Specific regulations depending on free zone registration |

**Key Resources:**
- [UAE Federal Data Protection Law](https://u.ae/en/about-the-uae/digital-uae/data/data-protection)
- [UAE E-Commerce Law Overview](https://www.government.ae/en/information-and-services/business/e-commerce)

### KSA — Expansion Market

| Regulation | Authority | Relevance |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| **Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL)** | SDAIA | KSA's comprehensive data protection law — effective September 2023 |
| **E-Commerce Law (2019)** | Ministry of Commerce | Regulates online marketplaces, electronic contracts, consumer protection |
| **Anti-Concealment Law** | Ministry of Commerce | Foreign ownership restrictions — requires local partnership structure |
| **Zakat & Tax** | ZATCA | VAT (15%) and corporate income tax considerations |

**Key Resources:**
- [Saudi PDPL Overview](https://sdaia.gov.sa/)
- [KSA E-Commerce Law](https://mc.gov.sa/en/pages/default.aspx)

### Jordan — Operations Hub

| Regulation | Authority | Relevance |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| **Cybercrime Law** | Ministry of Justice | Governs digital fraud, unauthorized access, data misuse |
| **E-Transactions Law** | Ministry of Digital Economy | Legal framework for electronic contracts and digital signatures |
| **Data Protection** | Various | Jordan follows EU-influenced data protection principles but has no comprehensive GDPR-equivalent |

### Egypt — Planned Market

## Advertising & Marketing Compliance

### Affiliate Marketing Specific Requirements

| Requirement | Description | Perff AI Compliance |
|-------------|-------------|-------------------|
| **Affiliate disclosure** | Publishers must disclose affiliate relationships to consumers | Publisher guidelines require disclosure; monitored via content review |
| **Truth in advertising** | Campaign claims must be accurate and substantiated | Campaign review process before launch |
| **Price accuracy** | Promoted prices must match actual merchant prices | Real-time price verification where available |
| **Intellectual property** | Publisher content must not infringe brand trademarks | Terms of service + content monitoring |
| **Prohibited products** | Cannot promote prohibited products (alcohol in KSA, gambling, etc.) | Country-specific campaign restrictions enforced at platform level |

### Country-Specific Advertising Rules

| Country | Key Rules |
|---------|-----------|
| **UAE** | National Media Council advertising standards; mandatory Arabic labeling; no misleading claims |
| **KSA** | Saudi FDA rules for health/food products; Sharia-compliant advertising; no alcohol/gambling |
| **Jordan** | Jordan Media Commission oversight; consumer protection advertising standards |
| **Egypt** | Supreme Council for Media Regulation; truth in advertising requirements |
