## Market Overview

The global affiliate marketing industry is experiencing rapid growth, projected to reach **$30B+ by 2031** ([Grand View Research](https://www.grandviewresearch.com/industry-analysis/affiliate-marketing-platform-market-report)). Within this, the **MENA region** represents one of the fastest-growing but most underserved markets — with booming e-commerce, rapidly digitizing populations, and virtually no dedicated regional affiliate infrastructure.

### Key Market Dynamics

| Factor | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Global affiliate marketing** | $17B (2023) → $30B+ (2031), CAGR ~12% |
| **MENA e-commerce GMV** | $37B (2023) → $57B+ (2027), CAGR ~15% ([Statista](https://www.statista.com/outlook/emo/ecommerce/mena)) |
| **MENA digital ad spend** | $5.4B (2023), growing 18% YoY ([WARC](https://www.warc.com/)) |
| **Affiliate share of digital marketing (global)** | 16% of all digital marketing revenue |
| **Affiliate share of digital marketing (MENA)** | ~5% — significant room for growth |
| **MENA internet penetration** | UAE 99%, KSA 98%, Egypt 72% — rapidly increasing |
| **MENA youth population** | 60% under 30 — digital-native, mobile-first consumers |

### Key Trends Driving Affiliate Growth in MENA

1. **Rising CAC on paid social** — Facebook/Instagram CPMs in UAE increased 40%+ (2022-2024), pushing brands to seek performance-based alternatives
2. **Coupon culture** — MENA consumers heavily use coupon/discount codes — ideal for affiliate distribution
3. **Influencer-to-affiliate shift** — Brands moving from CPM-based influencer deals to performance-tracked affiliate models
4. **Saudi Vision 2030** — Massive push for e-commerce and digital economy — KSA is investing billions in digital infrastructure
5. **Cash on delivery declining** — Digital payments growing, making online conversion tracking more reliable
6. **Cross-border e-commerce** — Temu, Trendyol, Shein entering MENA — all need local affiliate networks

4. **Full-stack approach** — Platform + managed services serves both self-serve SMBs and hands-off enterprises
