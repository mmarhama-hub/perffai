## The Problem

MENA's e-commerce market is growing at **15% annually** — faster than North America or Europe. Yet the region captures barely **2% of the $18B global affiliate marketing value**. This creates a massive structural gap:

### For Brands
- **80% of MENA brands** have zero structured affiliate programs
- Customer acquisition costs are **335× higher** than global affiliate benchmarks
- No reliable platform to discover, manage, and pay affiliate publishers
- Fraud rates in MENA digital advertising run **3–5× higher** than global averages

### For Publishers
- Zero reliable MENA-native platforms for monetization
- Payment delays of **60–120 days** are standard across the region
- No data transparency — publishers can't optimize what they can't measure
- Tools designed for Western markets fail on Arabic content, local payment rails, and regional consumer behavior

### The Market Gap
- Global affiliate marketing: **$18B** growing to **$30B** by 2031
- MENA's share: **<2%** despite having **7% of global e-commerce growth**
- No dominant affiliate platform exists in MENA — the market is fragmented across manual spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, and outdated tracking
