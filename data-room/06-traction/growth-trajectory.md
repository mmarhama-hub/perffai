## Key Growth Drivers

### What's Working
1. **Product-Market Fit** — 87% retention proves the solution works
2. **Network Effects** — More publishers attract more brands and vice versa
3. **Word-of-Mouth** — 64% of publishers join organically via referrals
4. **AI Differentiation** — 8.2% CVR (vs 1–3% industry) is a powerful selling point
5. **Client Expansion** — Existing clients increase spend 40% YoY on average

### Growth Constraints (That Investment Addresses)
1. **Sales capacity** — Only 2 people doing business development currently
2. **Technology** — AI features still in beta; full platform needs engineering investment
3. **Geographic** — Strong in UAE/KSA, but Egypt and wider GCC untapped
4. **Publisher tools** — Self-serve platform needed to scale publisher onboarding
5. **Marketing** — Near-zero marketing spend; growth entirely organic
