## Case Study 1: noon — E-Commerce Giant Scales Affiliate Revenue 4× in 12 Months

### Client Profile
| Detail | Value |
|---|---|
| **Brand** | noon |
| **Vertical** | E-Commerce Marketplace |
| **Markets** | UAE, KSA, Egypt |
| **Campaign Type** | CPS (Cost Per Sale) |
| **Partnership Start** | Q2 2023 |

### The Challenge
noon, MENA's largest locally-founded e-commerce marketplace, needed to diversify customer acquisition beyond paid social and search. Their existing affiliate efforts were fragmented across multiple networks with inconsistent tracking, high fraud rates (~12% of attributed conversions flagged as suspicious), and no unified performance optimization.

### The Solution
Perff AI deployed a full-stack affiliate program:
- **AI-powered publisher matching** — Connected noon with 120+ verified publishers specializing in UAE/KSA commerce
- **Fraud detection** — Implemented MENA-specific fraud filters reducing fraudulent conversions from 12% to <1%
- **Dynamic commission optimization** — Adjusted commission rates by category and publisher performance in real-time
- **Multi-vertical campaigns** — Electronics, Fashion, Grocery (noon minutes), Beauty

### Results (12-Month Performance)

| Metric | Before Perff AI | After 12 Months | Change |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Monthly Affiliate Revenue** | $85K | $340K | **+300%** |
| **Conversion Rate** | 2.1% | 8.4% | **+4× improvement** |
| **Fraud Rate** | 12% | <1% | **-92% reduction** |
| **Active Publishers** | 18 (fragmented) | 120+ (unified) | **+567%** |
| **ROAS** | 3.2× | 8.1× | **+153% improvement** |
| **Cost Per Acquisition** | $18.40 | $7.20 | **-61% reduction** |

### Key Takeaway
> *"Perff AI turned our affiliate channel into our most efficient acquisition source. The fraud detection alone saved us $40K+ monthly."*
