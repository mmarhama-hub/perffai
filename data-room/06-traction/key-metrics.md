## Metric Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **CVR** | Conversion Rate — percentage of clicks that result in a sale/action |
| **ROAS** | Return on Ad Spend — revenue generated per dollar of ad spend |
| **NRR** | Net Revenue Retention — revenue from existing clients year-over-year |
| **LTV** | Lifetime Value — total revenue expected from a client over their tenure |
| **CAC** | Customer Acquisition Cost — cost to acquire a new brand client |
| **MRR** | Monthly Recurring Revenue — predictable monthly revenue |
