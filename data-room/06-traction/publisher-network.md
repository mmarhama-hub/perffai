## Network Effects

Perff AI's publisher network creates a self-reinforcing growth loop:

```
More Publishers → More campaign options for brands
        ↓
More Brands join → More campaigns available for publishers
        ↓
Higher publisher earnings → Word-of-mouth referrals
        ↓
More Publishers join → Network effect compounds
```

### Evidence of Network Effects
- Publisher growth rate **accelerating** (not decelerating) despite larger base
- Cost to acquire new publishers **declining** — $42 in 2023 → $18 in 2025
- Brand close rate **increasing** — larger network is a stronger sales argument
- Average publisher earnings **growing** — more brands = more earning options
