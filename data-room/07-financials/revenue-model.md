## How Perff AI Makes Money

Perff AI operates a **performance-based marketplace model** where we earn revenue from every successful conversion processed through the platform. This is supplemented by emerging SaaS and managed services revenue.

---

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Performance Margin (Primary) — 90%+ of Revenue

**How it works:**

```
Brand pays $100 per conversion
        ↓
Perff AI keeps $25 margin (25% take rate)
        ↓
Publisher receives $75 payout
```

| Component | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Model** | Variable margin on every conversion |
| **Take Rate** | 20–30% (avg 25%) |
| **Pricing Types** | CPS (Cost Per Sale), CPA (Cost Per Action), CPL (Cost Per Lead) |
| **Status** | Live — primary revenue driver since 2022 |

**Revenue is driven by:**
- Volume of conversions processed through the platform
- Average commission value (varies by vertical: $3.50 food delivery → $180 real estate)
- Number of active campaigns
- Publisher performance optimization (higher CVR = more conversions)

### Margin by Vertical

| Vertical | Avg Brand Pays | Perff AI Margin | Publisher Receives | Typical Take Rate |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| E-Commerce (CPS) | 8–15% of sale | 2–3.5% of sale | 5.5–11.5% of sale | 25% |
| Food Delivery (CPA) | $6–8 per install | $1.50–$2.50 | $4–$5.50 | 28% |
| Real Estate (CPL) | $250–$400 per lead | $60–$120 | $140–$280 | 28% |
| Financial Services (CPL) | $80–$200 per lead | $18–$50 | $62–$150 | 24% |
| Fashion (CPS) | 10–15% of sale | 2.5–4% of sale | 7.5–11% of sale | 26% |
| Entertainment (CPA) | $5–$12 per sub | $1.20–$3 | $3.80–$9 | 24% |
