## Strategic Cost Principles

1. **Hire in low-cost MENA markets** — Amman, Cairo talent at 30–50% of UAE/KSA rates with high quality
2. **Keep variable costs aligned** — Publisher payouts scale with revenue, maintaining margin
3. **Invest heavily in engineering (2026–2027)** — Front-load AI development for long-term competitive advantage
4. **Scale marketing gradually** — Product-led growth first, then add marketing spend
5. **Path to profitability by 2028** — Clear line of sight without additional funding
