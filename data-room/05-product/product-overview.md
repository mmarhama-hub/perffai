# Product Overview

---

## What Perff AI Does

Perff AI is an **AI-powered affiliate marketing platform** that connects brands with affiliate publishers across MENA. The platform automates the entire affiliate lifecycle — from publisher discovery and campaign matching to real-time optimization, fraud detection, and payment processing.

### The Simple Explanation

Brands want customers. Publishers have audiences. Perff AI connects them — and uses AI to make sure the right publisher promotes the right brand to the right audience at the right time. Everyone pays and gets paid based on **real results only**.

---

## How It Works

### For Brands (Advertisers)

1. **Onboarding** — Brand creates account, sets campaign goals, defines target audience, sets commission rates
2. **Publisher Discovery** — Perff AI's AI recommends publishers whose audiences match the brand's target customers
3. **Campaign Launch** — Brand approves publishers, campaigns go live with tracking links generated automatically
4. **Optimization** — AI monitors performance in real-time, reallocates budget to highest-performing publishers
5. **Fraud Protection** — Multi-layer fraud detection filters invalid traffic (VPN, bots, click farms)
6. **Reporting** — Real-time dashboard shows conversions, revenue, ROAS, publisher performance
7. **Payment** — Brand pays Perff AI for verified conversions → Perff AI pays publishers

### For Publishers (Affiliates)

1. **Application** — Publisher applies via Perff AI platform with audience details
2. **Verification** — Perff AI screens for quality, content type, and audience relevance
3. **Campaign Discovery** — Publisher browses available campaigns, filtered by AI recommendations
4. **Link Generation** — One-click tracking link creation with deep linking and sub-ID support
5. **Promotion** — Publisher promotes via their channels (website, social media, email, etc.)
6. **Analytics** — Real-time dashboard shows clicks, conversions, earnings, and AI optimization tips
7. **Payment** — Reliable, on-time payments via bank transfer, PayPal, or local methods
