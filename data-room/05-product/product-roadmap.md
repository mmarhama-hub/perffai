## Revenue Model Evolution

| Phase | Model | Revenue per Client |
|---|---|---|
| **Current** (2024–2025) | Commission-based (% of attributed sales) | ~$24K/year ARR |
| **Hybrid** (2026) | Commission + SaaS subscription tiers | ~$30K/year |
| **Platform** (2027+) | SaaS (self-serve) + premium managed + API fees | ~$40K+/year |
