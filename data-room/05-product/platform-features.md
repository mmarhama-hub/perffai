# Platform Features — Complete Inventory

---

## Brand Features

### Campaign Management
- **Campaign Creator** — Set goals, budgets, commission rates, target audiences, and creative assets
- **Multi-Campaign Support** — Run multiple campaigns simultaneously across different verticals and markets
- **Budget Controls** — Daily/weekly/monthly caps; automatic pausing when budget exhausted
- **Commission Management** — Flexible commission structures (CPA, CPS, CPL, hybrid); tiered rates
- **Scheduling** — Campaign start/end dates; seasonal promotions (Ramadan, Black Friday, 11.11)
- **Geo-Targeting** — Target specific MENA markets (UAE, KSA, Egypt, Jordan, etc.)

### Publisher Management
- **Publisher Discovery** — AI-recommended publishers based on audience fit and historical performance
- **Application Review** — Review publisher applications with audience data, content samples, traffic stats
- **Approval Workflows** — Approve/reject publishers; bulk actions; category-based auto-approval
- **Publisher Tiers** — VIP, Premium, Standard tier system with differentiated commission rates
- **Communication** — In-platform messaging; campaign briefs; creative asset sharing

### Analytics & Reporting
- **Real-Time Dashboard** — Live conversions, revenue, clicks, ROAS, publisher performance
- **Campaign Analytics** — Drill down by campaign, publisher, geography, device, time period
- **Attribution Reports** — Last-click, first-click, multi-touch attribution models
- **Custom Reports** — Build custom reports with drag-and-drop; scheduled email delivery
- **Benchmarking** — Performance vs. industry benchmarks and category averages
- **Export** — CSV, Excel, PDF export for all reports

### Fraud Protection
- **Real-Time Monitoring** — Live fraud detection on all incoming traffic
- **MENA-Specific Models** — Trained on VPN masking, click farms, bot patterns unique to the region
- **Fraud Dashboard** — Visual overview of flagged traffic, blocked conversions, fraud types
- **Configurable Rules** — Set custom fraud thresholds per campaign or publisher
- **Automated Actions** — Auto-pause publishers with high fraud rates; auto-block IPs

## Publisher Features

### Campaign Discovery
- **Campaign Marketplace** — Browse available campaigns with filters (vertical, commission, market)
- **AI Recommendations** — Personalized campaign suggestions based on audience match
- **Campaign Details** — Full campaign briefs with creative assets, commission structures, terms
- **Wishlist** — Save campaigns for later; get notified of new campaigns in preferred verticals

### Tracking & Links
- **One-Click Link Generation** — Generate tracking links with sub-ID support
- **Deep Linking** — Link directly to product pages (not just homepages)
- **QR Codes** — Generate trackable QR codes for offline-to-online campaigns
- **Link Management** — Organize, label, and track all generated links
- **Short URLs** — Custom short URLs for cleaner sharing

### Earnings & Payments
- **Earnings Dashboard** — Real-time earnings with conversion details
- **Payment History** — Full payment history with invoices and receipts
- **Payment Methods** — Bank transfer, PayPal, local payment methods (CliQ, mada, etc.)
- **Payment Schedule** — Regular, on-time payments (Net-15; paid by 15th of following month)
- **Minimum Thresholds** — Low minimum payout thresholds for accessibility

### Performance Tools
- **AI Optimization Tips** — Personalized suggestions to improve conversion rates
- **A/B Testing** — Test different landing pages, copy, and creative per campaign
- **Performance Analytics** — Click-through rates, conversion rates, EPC, revenue trends
- **Audience Insights** — Understand which audience segments convert best
