## Executive Summary

**Perff AI** is an AI-powered affiliate marketing platform built specifically for the MENA region (Middle East and North Africa). The platform connects brands with affiliate publishers, automating campaign matching, fraud detection, and performance optimization across the region's fastest-growing digital commerce markets.

Often described as **"The Shopify of affiliate marketing for the Middle East,"** Perff AI addresses a critical infrastructure gap: while global affiliate networks like CJ Affiliate and Impact.com dominate Western markets, MENA — a region with $30B+ in e-commerce GMV ([Statista, 2024](https://www.statista.com/outlook/emo/ecommerce/mena)) — has lacked a dedicated, AI-native affiliate platform that understands local languages, payment methods, regulations, and consumer behavior.

**Key Highlights:**
- **870+ verified publishers** across MENA
- **$50M+ in attributed sales** processed through the platform
- **87% client retention rate** — vs. 65% industry average
- **8.2% conversion rate** — 3-8× above global affiliate industry average of 1-3%
- **50+ enterprise clients** including Noon, Talabat, DAMAC, Namshi, Trendyol, and more
- **35% year-over-year revenue growth**
- Active in **UAE, Jordan, KSA (launching), Egypt (planned)**

## Problem Statement

### The Broken State of Affiliate Marketing in MENA

Affiliate marketing in MENA is a **$1.5B opportunity** hampered by fragmentation, fraud, and lack of infrastructure:

| Problem | Evidence |
|---------|----------|
| **No regional platform** | Global networks (CJ, Impact) poorly serve MENA — limited Arabic support, no local payment rails, weak publisher networks in GCC |
| **Rampant fraud** | Up to **30% of affiliate clicks in MENA are fraudulent** — VPN masking, click farms, bot traffic, cookie stuffing ([DoubleVerify, 2023](https://doubleverify.com/global-insights-report/)) |
| **Fragmented publisher ecosystem** | Brands manually manage hundreds of publisher relationships across spreadsheets and WhatsApp |
| **Manual campaign management** | No automation for matching brands with the right publishers by vertical, geography, or audience |
| **Lack of attribution** | MENA's multi-channel shopping behavior (social → web → app → in-store) makes last-click attribution unreliable |
| **Payment complexity** | Cross-border payouts across UAE, KSA, Jordan, Egypt involve 5+ currencies and different regulatory regimes |

### Who Feels This Pain?
- **Brands** (e-commerce, fintech, food delivery, real estate) — Need reliable performance channels beyond paid ads
- **Publishers** (coupon sites, content creators, cashback platforms, influencers) — Want automated access to brand campaigns, fair attribution, and timely payouts
- **Agencies** — Struggle to scale affiliate programs manually across MENA

## Solution — Perff AI

Perff AI is a full-stack affiliate marketing platform combining marketplace, automation, AI, and analytics into a single regional solution.

### Core Capabilities

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **AI Campaign Matching** | ML models match brands with optimal publishers based on vertical, audience, geography, and historical performance |
| **Fraud Detection Engine** | Multi-layer AI fraud detection — VPN masking, bot clicks, click farms, geo-mismatch, cookie stuffing |
| **Predictive Campaign Scoring** | AI predicts campaign ROI before launch based on publisher mix, creative assets, and market timing |
| **Automated Reporting** | Real-time dashboards for brands and publishers — conversions, revenue, ROAS, CPA, publisher leaderboards |
| **Budget Optimization** | AI reallocates spend across publishers in real-time to maximize ROAS |
| **Publisher Marketplace** | Self-serve marketplace where publishers discover and join brand campaigns |
| **Cross-Border Payouts** | Automated multi-currency payouts across MENA (AED, SAR, JOD, EGP) |
| **Managed Services** | White-glove campaign management for enterprise brands |

### How It Works

**For Brands:**
1. **Launch** — Create a campaign with goals, budget, commission structure
2. **Match** — Perff AI automatically matches with optimal publishers from 870+ network
3. **Track** — Real-time attribution and conversion tracking with fraud filtering
4. **Optimize** — AI continuously reallocates budget to top-performing publishers
5. **Pay** — Automated performance-based payouts

**For Publishers:**
1. **Browse** — Discover campaigns matched to your audience on the marketplace
2. **Promote** — Get tracking links, creatives, and promotional materials
3. **Earn** — Commissions on verified conversions (CPA, CPS, CPL)
4. **Grow** — AI recommendations on which campaigns to promote and when

## Business Model

Perff AI generates revenue through three streams:

| Revenue Stream | Description | % of Revenue |
|----------------|-------------|--------------|
| **Performance Fees** | Commission on conversions generated through the platform (% of CPA/CPS paid by brands) | ~60% |
| **Platform / SaaS Fees** | Monthly subscription for brands to access the platform, analytics, and AI features | ~25% |
| **Managed Services** | Premium white-glove campaign management for enterprise clients | ~15% |

### Pricing Model
- **Performance Fee:** 15-25% of the brand's commission payout (e.g., if brand pays $10 CPA to publisher, Perff AI takes $1.50-$2.50)
- **SaaS Tier:** Starting at $500/month for self-serve, $2,000+/month for enterprise
- **Managed Services:** Custom pricing based on campaign scope and budget
