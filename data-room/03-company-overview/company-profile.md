## Company Description

Perff AI (operating under Perff Inc.) is an AI-powered affiliate marketing platform purpose-built for the Middle East and North Africa (MENA) region. The platform connects brands with affiliate publishers, automating campaign matching, fraud detection, and performance optimization to deliver measurable, performance-based marketing outcomes.

The company operates a two-sided marketplace:
- **Brand side:** Enterprise brands use Perff AI to discover, onboard, and manage affiliate publishers who promote their products and services on a performance (pay-per-result) basis.
- **Publisher side:** Content creators, coupon sites, comparison platforms, and social media influencers use Perff AI to discover brand campaigns, generate tracking links, and earn commissions on conversions they drive.
